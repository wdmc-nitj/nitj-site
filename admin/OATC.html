    Instructions for Issue of Duplicate Degree

    Issue of Duplicate Degree Certificate

    The duplicate Degree certificate is issued only if the original Degree certificate is really lost or stolen. 

    PROCEDURE:

    The following procedure is to be adopted by the candidate for getting a duplicate Degree    be issued from NIT Jalandhar: 
     

    • A student has to register an FIR on loss of detailed Marks Card/Semester Grade Report and Degree.
       
    • To advertise the loss in a National Daily after waiting for 15 days should apply with a copy of the newspaper cutting.
       
    • To submit an affidavit on Non-Judicial Stamp paper of Rs.10/- (see specimen).
       
    • To deposit/remit requisite fee in cash to the Cashier or through Bank Draft in favour of Registrar, NIT Jalandhar.
       
    • Write an application addressed to “The Assistant Registrar (Academics), NIT Jalandhar” mentioning the loss of your degree and reference of above documents.
       
    • Attach a Xerox copy of the original Degree conferred on you by NIT Jalandhar (if available).
       

    Duplicate Degree certificate will be issued by the Institute which will bear the Date of Re-Issue and the duplicate degree will be prepared as such as original and will carry the signatures of the authorities at the time of re-issuing the Duplicate Degree Certificate.

    Instructions for Issue of Duplicate Identity Card

    Issue of Duplicate Identity Card

    A duplicate Identity Card may be got re-issued from the Academic Branch in case the original Identity Card is lost or it gets damaged and becomes illegible. 

    PROCEDURE:

    (1.) In case, the Identity Card gets damaged, a student can apply for duplicate Identity Card. An application in this regard is to be made to Assistant Registrar (Academic) and the damaged Identity card is to be submitted along with the application.


    (2.) In case, the Identity Card is lost, the student has to make a report to the nearest Police Station and make an application for Duplicate Identity Card addressed to Assistant Registrar (Academic). (copy of the Police report is to be attached with the request). 

    PROCESSING FEE:
