GIVING BACK

Donate an amount for the following purpose as

  • Infrastructure
  • Facilities
  • Students (Scholarship, Best Student Awards, Gold Medal, etc... )
  • Academics
  • collaboration
  • Establishment Of centre of excellence
  • community outreach
  • Faculty welfare
  • Environmental Sustainability Program
  • Any other Activities

Utilization of fund

The fund mobilized from various sources and stakeholders will be utilized for various purposes, identified by the donor, as given below.

Infrastructure

Major

  • Alumni House Canteen: Min. Rs 250 Lakhs may be contributed towards the establishment of a Alumni Coffee House Canteen/Cafeteria in the campus. The house will be of the size of 2000 to 2500 sq feet area.

Minor

  • Vehicles for Institute such as bus, SUV, electric car, e-rickshaw for students in the campus etc: Min. an amount of Rs.10 Lakhs can be contributed towards the purchase of vehicle or a vehicle can be donated directly to the institute.

Facilities

  • Recreation facilities, Musical Instruments, Hobby club facilities: Min amount of Rs. 2 Lakhs may be contributed for recreation facility development for the students.
  • Purchase of assets/ equipment/ machines/software: Min. amount of Rs. 5 Lakhs may be contributed towards the purchase of above items or other items, costing not less than 1 Lakh, can also be directly donated to the institute/department.

Students

  • Entrepreneurship program: To promote the entrepreneurial activities in the campus a min. amount of Rs. 3 Lakhs can be funded to the students for their entrepreneurial endeavours.
  • Establishment and maintenance of Tinkering lab: Tinkering labs can be identified by donors and funded totally towards their establishments or a Min. amount of Rs 10 Lakhs may be donated to a department towards its establishment.
  • Semester, yearly or full-term tuition fee for B. Tech,/M.Tech/ Ph.D students, with poor economic background, as the per present fee structure given at institute website.
  • Financial support to students for attending national/ international events related to their research activities: Min. amount of Rs. 50,000 , may be donated towards the promotion and encouragement of students to present their research work at national and international event.
  • B.Tech./M.Tech./ Research scholar project funding: A minimum amount of Rs. 2 Lakhs may be donated which will be utilized to fund the B.Tech. (a batch of four students), M. Tech or Ph.D. students, project/research work.
  • Support for patent filing: A min. amount of 1 Lakh, may be contributed for filing of patents of students
  • Scholarship, Best Student Awards, Gold Medal, etc...

Academics

  • Lecture Series from person of eminence:A Min.amount of one Lakh, may be contributed which will be utilized to invite the renowned national and international experts of various fields for their lecture or a lecture series
  • Equipment, hardware, software and other facilities to the exiting laboratories of the departments in the institute: The instrument/hardware/software useful to a department/laboratory/cell of the institute may be identified and funded or a min. contribution of an amount of Rs. 2 Lakhs may be made. Equipment can be also donated directly to the respective department by the donor.
  • Establishment of new laboratories: Laboratory to be established can be identified after discussion with the Institute/Department and the full or partial fund for its establishment can be donated.
  • Organizing workshops/ seminars/training programmes: A full or partial funding for organizing these events can be made or a minimum contribution of Rs. 2 Lakhs per activity may made towards the organization of these programme.

Collaboration

  • Industrial collaboration for research/ consultancy/ Training etc.
  • Interdisciplinary research

Collaboration can be done with the institute or department or with the identified faculty of the institute with detailed terms and conditions specifying the objective and contribution towards that reason.

Establishment of Centre of Excellence

Funds for the department of center of excellence can be given to the institute which will be utilised for developing its infrastructure, manpower, software and hardware. For the establishment of a Chair in the institute a min. amount of Rs. 25 Lakhs per chair can be contributed.

Innovative programmes including research and extension work

Support for development of innovation lab or special workshop for the students may be taken up. The minimum funds required will be 10 lakhs for general equipment. A dedicated facility may also be created which may be named after an individual or group. However, this will include building, furniture and equipments. The approximate cost may range from 30 lakhs to One crores.

Community Outreach Activities

Students of the Institute with the support from the Institute have started a project called “PRAYASH” to teach the underprivileged kids from nearby localities. The minimum contribution will be 1 lakhs. Further donation in terms of kinds as direct purchase & supply can be taken up.

Faculty Welfare

  • Support for attending conferences/ seminars/ other academic activities: Min. amount of Rs. 50,000 per faculty per event
  • Young Faculty Research Fellowships: Min. amount of Rs. 2 Lakh/Year.
  • Support for industrial interaction: Min. amount of Rs. 1 Lakh/faculty/ Month+ Rs. 50,000/-(TA/DA)
  • Faculty benevolent fund: Any amount not less than 1 Lakh

Environmental Sustainability Program

  • Rain water harvesting
  • Tree Plantation
  • Any other area identified by person/organization
  • Any other Activity deemed fit from time to time as proposed by alumni/organization.
