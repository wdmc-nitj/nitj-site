Beautiful campus
The Campus of NITJ is quite beautiful. There is enough greenery in the campus. It has a big ground, basketball court, volleyball court, badminton courts in hostels. The IT building and administrative blocks are also good to look at. Hostel facility is good food is also good. There are a total 6 small hostels along with 3 mega hostels 3 are under construction. There are separate mess for vegetarian and non vegetarian students and they have option as well special food is offered twice a week. The campus has a very large gym as well.
Campus Tour
Cultural & Technical Fest
Utkansh & Techniti, the cultural & technical fests of NITJ attract colleges from the whole of North India. You get a lot of opportunity to participate in various fun & technical activities during these fests.
Utkansh
Placements
The placements at NITJ are comparatively good for most of the branches. CS had the best placements followed by mechanical and even textile technology. So even if you are an average student you can get decent enough placement. Around 70% of students get placed in my course the highest package was 45 LPA offered by Microsoft and the lowest package was 4 LPA Average package was 8.57 in 2020 Microsoft, amazon, Deloitte, optum, Reliance Life industries, Samsung, Honda, adobe etc.

No Ragging
There is almost no ragging at NITJ. There is separate hostel for 1st year students to stop ragging.
