Introduction

To develop technical human resource of excellence suitable for global requirements. To ensure good quality academic and industrial research programmes in different areas of engineering and technology. To generate industry-institute synergy for shaping technical education to meet the requirement of industry.

Aim and Objectives

  • 1. To develop intrapersonal and interpersonal skills.
  • 2. To promote an in-depth understanding of soft skills and English language that lies beyond the ken of the syllabus.
  • 3. To provide a platform to students for mutual exchange of ideas, knowledge and skills through ingenious and captivating monthly group activities such as group discussion, debate, quiz, documentary review, movie review, and role play.
  • 4. To encourage the students to display their extra-curricular skills that would promote a healthy holistic development of the student.
  • 5. To arrange competitions like elocution contest, essay writing, book review giving them an opportunity for self-expression.
  • 6. To incorporate speech delivery enhancement activities.
  • 7. To instill in the minds of the students the spirit of self-help, service sacrifice and cooperation.
