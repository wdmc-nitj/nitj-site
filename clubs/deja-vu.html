Introduction


"We are a bunch of movie enthusiasts aiming to spread our predilection towards movies and to resolve the enigma related to movie and video making. We provide people a platform to create something prodigious from nothing. We look for people who are ardent towards movies, be it direction, writing scripts, or editing. We conduct workshops throughout the year to train these people for their passions. "
